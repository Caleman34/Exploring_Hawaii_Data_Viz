Discussion

  • How did this idea come about? We wanted to do something fun and relaxing, something to look foward to in the future (because I KNOW we all need a Hawaii/Tropical vacation soon)

  • We bounced tons of ideas off each other and narrowed it down to more ideal data that would be sought out while planning a trip.

Challenges and Victories

  • Victory- Finding the exact data our project needed

  • Challenges in making the Flask/ SQLAlchey and SQLite connection. Multiple errors, our findings are that there is a deep disconnect that requires more time to research. Back up solution is to convert our data into a csv or Json file.

  • Victory-creating our SQL database with multiple CVSs.

  • Challenges-Even though we have worked with different branches before, the more data we had , the more diffuculty we had pushing, merging.

  • Challenge- API connections were not consistent, CORS error was unable to be resolved

  • Victory- Leaflet map layers, legend, and overflow contained to designated box and working properly

  • Challenge- images did not work across all platforms- needed to change from .jfiff to .png

Final Thoughts

At first glance, you're probably thinking "how is this different from other vacation planning websites?" Our web page is unique. We have incorpated real time APIs, used Leaflet to give you ideal hotel locations and what your surroundings will be like.
